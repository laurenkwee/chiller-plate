Who We Are

We are five highschool students from Hawai'i that are passionate about doing volunteer work to protect the environment. From our personal experiences, we realized that many high school students have difficulty finding volunteer organizations that they are passionate about. We created AltruCliq to connect volunteer events with potential student volunteers in order to build a better community.
